Lesson 2 of 5 • 5 min read
What is APR?
APR (Annual Percentage Rate) is the yearly cost of borrowing money on your credit card. It includes the interest rate and any fees.
Key Point: A higher APR means you pay more interest on your balance. That's why paying off your balance quickly is important.
How Interest Works
If you carry a balance, interest is calculated daily and added to your balance. This is why carrying a balance is expensive.
